Team Project of 5

Witch's Winter

Position Visual Artist
Tools Figma, Unity
Genre 2D Side-scrolling Platformer
Duration 15 weeks

Concept Statement

An exploration of environment-driven puzzle design where seasonal transformations shape player interaction and progression.

01 Project Overview

Witch's Winter is a 2D side-scrolling puzzle-platformer where players explore four seasonal environments to recover lost magic and restore balance to the world. Each season introduces unique environmental interactions that influence how players navigate obstacles and solve puzzles.

The gameplay focuses on using seasonal abilities to manipulate the environment, encouraging players to think strategically as they progress through different areas of the game.

This project is currently under development. The portfolio will be updated with final gameplay, systems, and design outcomes upon completion.
